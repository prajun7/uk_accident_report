Big Data Project: UK Traffic Accident Analysis
Step 1: Business Case Evaluation

BUSINESS QUESTION: 
How can local transportation authorities predict the likely severity of a traffic accident (e.g., Fatal/Serious vs. Slight) based strictly on observable environmental and road conditions (such as speed limit, road type, lighting, weather, and time of day), and use this information to proactively optimize emergency responder deployments and adjust dynamic speed limits?

LIFE-CHANGING SOLUTION:
By predicting which specific combinations of road conditions, times, and geographic locations probabilistically result in "Fatal or Serious" accidents, authorities can:
1. Preventative Measures: Deploy variable message signs (VMS) alerting drivers to slow down during specific identified high-risk weather/time combinations.
2. Resource Allocation: Stage emergency response vehicles (ambulances, police) closer to identified high-risk zones during peak expected hours.
3. Infrastructure Investment: Identify consistently dangerous road configurations that need proactive redesign or enhanced nighttime lighting.
