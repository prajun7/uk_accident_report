Big Data Project: UK Traffic Accident Analysis
Step 2: Data Identification

We already possess the necessary dataset to address our business question. The data reflects a substantial corpus of historical UK Department for Transport (DfT) Traffic Accident data from 2005 to 2015. 

DATASET STRUCTURE:
The data is provided in the current environment across three primary CSV files:

1. Accidents0515.csv (~244MB)
   - Contains core geographic, ambient, and overarching facts about every recorded accident. 
   - Key attributes: Accident_Index, Accident_Severity (Our Target Variable), Speed_limit, Light_Conditions, Weather_Conditions, Time/Date, Location.

2. Vehicles0515.csv (~206MB)
   - Details regarding the vehicles involved (Age, type, maneuvers).
   - Links to accidents via Accident_Index.

3. Casualties0515.csv (~108MB)
   - Details the demographics and severities of damage down to individual passengers.
   - Links to accidents via Accident_Index.

Given constraints our hands are largely locked to this dataset, but it is deeply rich enough to fulfill the business case.
